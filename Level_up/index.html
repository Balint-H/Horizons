Need a leg up? Level the playing field

Level up is a table football table with motorised legs. Our goal is to provide a more dynamic playing experience, by tilting the table towards the player clearly in the lead; creating a more challenging and enjoyable experience to all. Not to mention it can balance the table on an uneven surface.
