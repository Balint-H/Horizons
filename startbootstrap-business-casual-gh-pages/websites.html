Website made using Start Bootstrap

Start Bootstrap was picked for its easy to use, modular themes, as well as its extensive tutorials. Notepad++ was used to edit .css and .html files. Git and Github was used for version control.


How to Register a Domain?

What makes a Good Website?

Consistent style, easily navigateable UI, multiplatform support are the most important design points in my opinion.


Visual and Audio feedback for interactive elements, calm, non highly-stimulating UI. Audio feedback was not implemented due to the limited scope of the assignment. A sans-serif font was chosen for readability. Colors were tested with different color-blindness simulating filters to ensure all informance is conveyed to people with the three most common family of colourblindness. Alt text was added for images for people using screen readers.


GDPR IS IMPORTANT


Platforms such as Google adwords provide easy ways of targeted advertising. Collaboration/giveaways with social influencers is a good way to reach an audience, as long as the channel's viewers would be interested in the product.
